Jigsaw PC

Jigsaw PC is a 1st PC method, two Perfect Clear openers specialized for consistency and increased general scoring from 1st PC. It was devised by stickmancomic. The PC chances depend on the variant used, with either 69.88% or 74.52%, which is comparable to PCO’s 61.19% or 85.36% with I-piece held.

For Ultra or Blitz, this 1st PC method has advantages over PCO: extra score gain from a T-Spin Zero, guaranteed 2nd PC in case the 1st PC fails, higher chances for saving the T-piece for 2nd PC (leading to better 2nd PC chances), and more intuitive solves when compared to PCO. The method’s disadvantages are more noticeable in all other gamemodes, so it is not recommended outside of Ultra or Blitz.

First bag

Jaws Variant, T-piece in Hold

PCO Variant, J/L-piece in Hold

Either of the two variants can always be built on 1st bag.

The Jaws variant is possible to build if either the S-piece comes before T or J, or the Z-piece comes before T or L. This happens 86.67% of time.

The PCO variant is possible to build if T, J and L all come before S and Z, the build is mirrored according to which of S/Z comes first. This happens 80% of the time.

2nd PC Recovery

On queues with no solution, the T/J/L-piece is placed normally and the “Tub” setup is built. This 2nd PC setup can always be built in this way. It is advised to save the I-piece for a 57.14% Tetris PC chance, which may compensate for the score loss from missed 1st PC.

There is a 100% PC chance, with 7 solutions that can be looked up here.

The pieces saved from this 2nd PC are O, S, Z and I, which are good for DPC followups.

An alternative 2nd PC recovery setup can be built that is much simpler for solving and effectively works the same as the previous setup with saved I-piece. This setup can also always be built.

There is a 100% PC chance, with 2 solutions.

The pieces saved from this setup are S/Z and O. Again, this is good for DPC followups.
