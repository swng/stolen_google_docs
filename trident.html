second bag

   

In the second bag, you can always stack this build to perform a TST. The S will require 180 to place if L and I are placed beforehand. Gravity may be required to place the L.

third bag

   

                  100% build                                     optimal build

In the third bag, you can always stack the first build to perform a TST. However, it is recommended to go for the optimal build whenever possible as it will always lead to a clean fourth bag stack. The L will require 180 to place if Z is placed beforehand.

fourth bag

In the fourth bag, you can always perform a TST. You should need only one build, but the stack is left in an atrocious state for the guaranteed build. As such, it is recommended to use alternative builds when possible.
