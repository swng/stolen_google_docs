Probe Cannon

Probe Cannon is a 100% TST Tower continuation to Trustworthy DT / Snail TKI found by ReallyNotSomeone.

First Bag

1st bag is Trustworthy DT or Snail TKI. 40.8% build rate including mirrors.

Second Bag

2nd bag is 100% with 2 congruents. Hold O, 1st image T<L, 2nd image L<T.

Then, there are 2 routes to cover all 3rd bags.

Third Bag Route 1 S<Z

Try to delay TST to combo with 2nd TST.

14L PC Cont (100%)

This is 100% build rate with LJO interchangeable. Z is kept on hold.

You now have an 100% chance to PC with 4 minimals. When to use the solves are also explained. First, determine which piece is last out of TLJZ:

T/J last; hold T/J, 1st solve S<L, 2nd solve L<S:

L/Z last; hold L/Z, 1st solve T<J, 2nd solve J<T:

Third Bag Route 2 Z<S

Again, try to delay TST to combo with 2nd TST.

14L PC Cont (99.6%)

This is 100% build rate. Keeping Z on hold barely increases PC%, so Z is just placed.

You now have a 99.48% chance to PC with 15 minimals.

Easier 14L PC Cont (92.1%)

This is also 100% build rate with LJO interchangeable. Z is kept on hold. It is a much lower chance than the one above but also has much less minimals. LJ fixed, 1st solve covers nearly 90% (89.92%) of queues.

C-Spin Cont

Although it does not have an easy 100% 14L, Z<S has the advantage of being able to construct a C-Spin afterwards without wasting a T. Below is an example for T/J<L/O, if you can’t build it then just freestyle.
